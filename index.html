New Harbor Development is actively seeking acquisition opportunities in the Mid-Atlantic region that align with its mission. We are interested in acquisitions of existing mixed income, income restricted, and naturally affordable properties. We are also interested in new construction and land development opportunities in transit oriented areas and areas of opportunity.

If you have an opportunity that you would like to share with New Harbor Development please email acquisitions@newharbordev.com
